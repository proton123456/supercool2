      Count Speed 3D

      Count Speed 3D is a racing game in which you have to drive through the right gates to gain more speed for your final sprint. Score the highest points multiplier possible!

      How to play Count Speed 3D?

      In this 3D game, you can make your car travel faster by collecting the numbers displayed on the blue games. Drive through the forcefield and fill the speedometer. At the end of the track, your car will expend all the speed you’ve collected by making a final sprint down the colorful score multiplier lane.

      Watch out for the obstacles though! The traffic cones and light red barriers will slow you down. The dark red and navy blue obstacles will stop your car altogether.

      You can collect coins to boost your car’s starting stats, such as your overall speed and the amount of profit you make from completing each race. You can also save up points to unlock new cars via the shop menu.

      Game Controls

      • Use the MOUSE to play
      • HOLD and SWIPE left or right to guide the car past the obstacles
